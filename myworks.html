VeriPolicy

AI & Strategic Analytics

AI-powered strategic policy simulator and tracker built to help analysts evaluate cross-domain impacts of defence, climate, and strategic technology policies. Uses anomaly detection and RAG pipelines over global datasets to write structured briefs automatically.

  • Local RAG: sentence-transformers embedded index inside CPU ChromaDB.
  • LLM Generator: Llama 3.3 70B via Groq generates structured 4-section Foresight Memos.
  • News Fetcher: RSS parser pulls real policy briefs from AP & Reuters RSS feeds.
